About the Society

The Honor Society of the Philippines is the first and only honor society of the Philippines that espouses the University of the Philippines values of integrity, excellence, and service. It is a national academic society dedicated to recognizing outstanding achievement and advancing the role of scholars and civic-minded citizens in nation-building.

Founded by members of the University of the Philippines academic community and inspired by the legacy of Phi Kappa Phi Chapter 045, established in 1933, the Society is Filipino in spirit, grounded in history, and responsive to the needs of the nation and its people.

Mission & Core Values

The mission of the Honor Society of the Philippines is to build a community of scholars and leaders who understand that scholarship is not an end in itself, but a responsibility. The Society affirms that honor is measured not by prestige or applause, but by service to the Filipino people.

In an age marked by division and intolerance, the Society upholds scholarship that places truth above ego, humility above self-promotion, and responsibility above recognition.

Excellence

Excellence calls for disciplined pursuit of truth, intellectual rigor, and lifelong learning. It is guided by humility, the recognition that knowledge is always incomplete and that intellectual gifts are entrusted to us for purposes greater than ourselves.

Integrity

Integrity demands honesty in thought and action, courage to admit mistakes, patience to listen, and the discipline to place truth above ego. It is the commitment to choose what is right even when it is inconvenient or unseen.

Service

Service gives meaning to both excellence and integrity. It is the expression of a servant-hearted spirit that places the needs of others above one’s own and applies knowledge for the common good, especially in service of the marginalized and unheard.

Guided by these values, members of the Honor Society of the Philippines are expected to be diligent, compassionate, and grounded; to lead with humility; and to measure honor not by status, but by character and service to the nation.

Membership

Membership in the Honor Society of the Philippines is by invitation. Eligibility and selection are based on demonstrated academic distinction, professional merit, and sustained contributions consistent with the standards of the Society. Nomination and selection processes are governed by policies approved by the Society’s governing body and are conducted with due regard for fairness, deliberation, and institutional integrity. Categories of membership may include founding members, charter members, fellows, and honorary members, as formally defined by the Society.
